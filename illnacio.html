~Jx! Illnacio Comics

Illnacio was the former name for everything related to comics and illustrations created by ~Jx!. But I ended up having trouble giving myself an identity under such nickname, because I also wanted to provide other forms of content and Illnacio felt too restrictive for that.

Here you will be able to find everything related to comics created by ~Jx/Illnacio, which is a merge of the old Illnacio concept with the current ~Jx! Hub. Here you'll find comics such as The Heibin and Ducky Show, Heibin & Ducky and others.

The Heibin and Ducky Show

Volume 1 (es) | Volume 2 (es) | Volume 3 (es) | Volume 4 (es)

The Heibin and Ducky Show is a four volume special introducing to the Heibin and Ducky universe. Throughout these pages you will find a series of random short tales which will give you a glimpse of each character's personality.

The Volumes are released in both Spanish and English, through PDF files that you can read here online, through your browser, or download for offline access later.
